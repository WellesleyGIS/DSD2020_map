As part of Digital Scholarship Day 2020, the DS Day team sent out a survey to members of the Wellesley community asking questions about what they see and hear outside of their windows in order to create a map of what Wellesley looks and sounds like all around the world.

Click on the pins on the map to see asnwers from all over the global Wellesley community!

Note:
The data contained in this map will not be used outside of this map, and are stored in a spreasheet that only the DS Day team has access to.

This map will remain available online through September 1, 2020. If you wish to remove your data from this map before that date, please contact the DS Day team at: dsday2020@wellesley.edu
